Create Reminders to Avoid Tickets.

Denver gives you a ticket if you don't move your car according to their schedule. There seems to be no easy way to create a reminder for the "3rd Wednesday of the month" in any calendar app I had, so I was happy to find that the city offers to send text message reminders via an online app. This worked great for a few weeks, until it did not, and I found myself with another ticket. Since the city assumes no responsibility for failure of the service it provides, I decided to just make my own service. Hypocritically, it too will assume no liability, because I don't have the money for that.
