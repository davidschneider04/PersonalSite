Create Reminders to Avoid Tickets.

Denver gives you a ticket if you don't move your car according to their schedule. I was happy to find that the city offers to send text message reminders. This worked great for a few weeks, until it did not, and I found myself with another ticket, and an explanation that the city assumes no responsibility for failure of the service it provides. So, I made my own solution. It's just a set of calendar events, but I couldn't find this level of customizability within any calendar apps. Hypocritically, it too will assume no liability for its failure, because I don't have the money for that. Link to source code..
