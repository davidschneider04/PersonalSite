A Monthly Budget Template.

I made this because I was frustrated with traditional finance tracking apps like Mint and wanted something that let me work at a super granular, customizable level and also let me track at a glance how I was doing across whatever categories I'd created. The basic idea is that once you have it set up, you just enter each individual expense in the "Transactions" page. These are automatically grouped and graphed in the "Trajectory" tab, where you can see the pacing of your current total expenses for the month against the other categories. This pacing is customizable by category-- for example, the pacing budget for my "Bars/Alcohol" category is proportionally increased for Friday and Saturday each week, since that is typically when I find myself making those purchases.
